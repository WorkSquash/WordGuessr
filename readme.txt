
Welcome to WordGuessr!
You will choose a category and try to guess the word by suggesting letters or the whole word.
You have a limited number of tries, so choose wisely.
Good luck!

P.S: 
Hey, I forgot to mention but you can add your own words to the game by editing the custom_words.txt file in the wordList folder.
To play with your custom wordlist just select the Custom Words category.
